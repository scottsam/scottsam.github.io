Better Health Nigeria

It has been estimated that Nigeria has only about 27 physicians for every 100,000 people living in Nigeria, against the WHO standard of 100 physicians per 100,000 due to various factors bordering from inadequate healthcare infrastructures and corruption deeply rooted in the Nigerian system. Due to this, a lot of Patients in Nigeria are left unattended to, Or rather, not properly attended to due to the pressures arising from this imbalance.
